Welcome to Rice Plant Disease Detection

This project helps farmers detect diseases in rice plants using machine learning. It can identify various diseases such as bacterial leaf blight, brown spot, leaf blast, and more. By early detection, farmers can take necessary measures to protect their crops and improve yield.

After predicting the rice plant disease based on the uploaded image, the application can take appropriate measures such as suggesting specific treatments, recommending relevant agricultural practices, or providing information about the disease's impact on crop yield. This can assist farmers in making informed decisions to mitigate the disease and ensure better crop health
